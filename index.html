WHAT WE DO

Advanced Multidisciplinary Services

Our highly trained team combines evidence-based physiotherapy, active rehabilitation, and complementary therapies to support your complete recovery.

Orthopedic & Sports Physiotherapy

Evidence-based recovery plans for sprains, muscle strains, ligament tears (ACL, rotator cuff), running injuries, and chronic pain. Hands-on joint mobilizations and customized active strengthening plans.

Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy

Specialized assessment and rehabilitation for pelvic girdle pain, incontinence, prenatal/postnatal recovery, organ prolapse, and muscle dysfunction. Conducted in fully private, comfortable environments.

Registered Massage Therapy (RMT)

Targeted deep tissue, myofascial release, Swedish massage, and trigger point therapy to reduce chronic tension, improve localized circulation, and speed up overall muscular recovery.

Acupuncture & Dry Needling

Therapeutic insertion of needles targeting neurological pathways and muscular trigger points to immediately alleviate chronic pain, stimulate blood flow, and relieve severe muscle spasms.

Concussion & Vestibular Rehab

Advanced diagnostic and active balancing programs for vertigo (BPPV), persistent dizziness, post-concussive syndrome, and general gait/coordination instability issues.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured, protocol-driven physical therapy following orthopedic surgeries (knee/hip replacements, ACL reconstructions, shoulder operations) to safely restore full range of motion and joint stability.
