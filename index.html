OUR CLINIC PHILOSOPHY

Rehabilitation Focused on Long-Term Results

At Prime Physiotherapy, we believe in a transparent and patient-centered approach. Our clinic stands out because our therapists prioritize active, hands-on treatment. We do not keep you booked for sessions you do not need, nor do we run a "volume-first" model.

Our clinic director, Ali Faghani, and our entire multidisciplinary team of FCAMPT physiotherapists, pelvic floor health specialists, and registered massage therapists collaborate to diagnose the root cause of your pain and deliver structured, sustainable exercises to keep you recovered.
